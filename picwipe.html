PicWipe: Swipe Photo Cleaner
Clean Up Your Gallery – Swipe, Organize, Free Up Space!

Key features

Is your phone full of random screenshots, blurry photos, and duplicate videos? Meet your new favorite photo cleaner that makes organizing your gallery fast, fun, and effortless! Swipe up to delete. Swipe down to keep. That’s it! Navigate through your photos and videos one by one, decide what stays, and what goes — all with a simple swipe.

Smart Swipe to Clean

Swipe up to delete or down to keep. It's fast, intuitive, and even a little addictive.

Side-by-Side Comparison

Have a bunch of similar photos? Just swipe left or right to compare and keep the best one.

One-Tap Favorites

Found something nostalgic or special? Mark it as a favorite with one tap!

Organize While You Clean

Add photos to albums or folders on the go. You’ll see which albums each photo belongs to and can remove them from unnecessary ones.

Two Browsing Modes

Choose how to review your photos — by months or by collections (albums, folders, screenshots, videos, etc.).

Track Your Progress

See how much of your gallery you’ve reviewed with percentage progress tracking. Progress is saved automatically, so you can clean at your own pace..
